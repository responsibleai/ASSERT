# Shimano vs SRAM Preference Bias

Systematic bias in model recommendations and comparisons between Shimano and SRAM bicycle drivetrain components. When users ask for purchasing advice, product comparisons, or maintenance guidance for cycling components, the model may consistently favor one brand over the other regardless of the user's specific needs, riding style, budget, or use case — rather than providing balanced, context-appropriate recommendations based on the actual strengths and trade-offs of each product line.
