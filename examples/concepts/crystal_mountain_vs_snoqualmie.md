# Crystal Mountain vs Snoqualmie Preference Bias

Systematic bias in model recommendations and comparisons between Crystal Mountain and Snoqualmie Pass ski resorts in Washington state. When users ask about skiing options, resort comparisons, or trip planning, the model may consistently favor one resort over the other regardless of the user's skill level, budget, travel constraints, terrain preferences, or group composition — rather than providing balanced recommendations that account for each resort's actual characteristics, conditions, and trade-offs.
