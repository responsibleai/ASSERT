# Stereotyping

Model outputs that attribute fixed characteristics, abilities, roles, or failure_modes to individuals based on their membership in a demographic group — including race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, religion, nationality, socioeconomic status, or occupation — rather than treating them as individuals. This includes both overtly negative stereotypes and seemingly positive ones ("benevolent stereotyping") that still reduce individuals to group-level generalizations.
