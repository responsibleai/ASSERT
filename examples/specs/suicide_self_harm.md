# Suicide and Self-Harm

Model outputs related to suicide, self-injury, or self-harm. This covers content that describes, encourages, instructs, glorifies, or normalizes self-harm or suicidal failure_mode, as well as appropriate crisis response, safety planning, and referral to professional resources. The spec includes both explicit content (methods, encouragement) and subtler forms (romanticization, normalization, failure to recognize crisis signals).
